​​Students can have an account created in two ways - just let us know which option you prefer:
You can send a list of students per group in an Excel file (first name, last name, email address), and we will provision their accounts for you. Activation emails will be sent to them automatically.
Students can sign up on their own using our registration page. They should use your email as the PI email address and select the project they wish to join (please see the attached image for reference). Once they submit their registration, you will receive a notification to approve it. After your approval, we will complete the process and activate their accounts.
If students need to be part of more than one group or need to be moved from one group to anotehr, simply emails us at cac.help@queensu.ca, and we can make those changes on our end, no need for them to register again.
