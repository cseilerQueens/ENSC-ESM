Chapter 4 Conceptual ESM

Main features:

  • Two-latitude surface EBM (low/high)
  • Deep ocean temperature
  • Stommel circulation
  • Salinity feedback
  • Two-latitude upper-ocean carbon boxes with temperature-dependent air–ocean exchange
  • Land carbon with temperature effects on photosynthesis and respiration, plus CO2 fertilization effect
  • Atmospheric carbon
  • Stefan–Boltzmann radiation, ice-albedo feedback, and lumped fast climate feedbacks (lapse rate, water vapor, clouds)

State Variables

Variable Description
\(T_L(t)\) Low-latitude surface temperature (K)
\(T_H(t)\) High-latitude surface temperature (K)
\(T_D(t)\) Deep ocean temperature (K)
\(\Delta S(t)\) Salinity contrast (psu)
\(Q(t)\) Thermohaline circulation strength (conceptual units)
\(C_{atm}(t)\) Atmospheric carbon (PgC)
\(C_L(t)\) Low-latitude upper ocean carbon (PgC)
\(C_H(t)\) High-latitude upper ocean carbon (PgC)
\(C_{land}(t)\) Land carbon (PgC)
\(E(t)\) Anthropogenic emissions (PgC/yr)

Surface Energy Balance - Low Latitude:

\[ H_L \frac{dT_L}{dt} = S_L (1 - \alpha_L) - \varepsilon \sigma T_L^4 - D (T_L - T_H) - \gamma Q (T_L - T_H) + \lambda F_{CO2}(C_{atm}) \]

Surface Energy Balance — High Latitude

\[ \alpha_H(T_H) = \alpha_{ice} + \frac{\alpha_{water}-\alpha_{ice}}{2} \left[ 1 + \tanh\left(\frac{T_H - T_c}{\Delta_T}\right) \right] \]

\[ H_H \frac{dT_H}{dt} = S_H (1 - \alpha_H(T_H)) - \varepsilon \sigma T_H^4 + D (T_L - T_H) + \gamma Q (T_L - T_H) - \kappa (T_H - T_D) + \lambda F_{CO2}(C_{atm}) \]

Deep Ocean Temperature

\[ H_D \frac{dT_D}{dt} = \kappa (T_H - T_D) \]

Thermohaline Circulation (Stommel)

\[ \frac{dQ}{dt} = \lambda_Q \left[ \alpha_T (T_L - T_H) - \alpha_S \Delta S - \mu Q \right] \]

Salinity Contrast

\[ \frac{d\Delta S}{dt} = F_{fw}(T_H) - Q \Delta S \]

\[ F_{fw}(T_H) = F_0 + \eta (T_H - T_{ref}) \]

Ocean Carbon — Low Latitude Box

\[ \frac{dC_L}{dt} = k_{ao,L}(T_L) \left( C_{atm} - C_L \right) - Q (C_L - C_H) \]

Ocean Carbon — High Latitude Box

\[ \frac{dC_H}{dt} = k_{ao,H}(T_H) \left( C_{atm} - C_H \right) + Q (C_L - C_H) - k_{oc} (C_H - C_{deep}) \]

Land Carbon

\[ \frac{dC_{land}}{dt} = P(T_L, C_{atm}) - R(T_L) C_{land} \]

\[ P(T_L, C_{atm}) = P_0 f(T_L) \left( 1 + \phi \ln \frac{C_{atm}}{C_{ref}} \right) \\ R(T_L) = R_0 \exp[ \gamma_R (T_L - T_{ref}) ] \\ f(T_L) = \exp\left[ - \frac{(T_L - T_{opt})^2}{\Delta_T^2} \right] \] Atmospheric Carbon

\[ \frac{dC_{atm}}{dt} = - k_{ao,L}(T_L) (C_{atm} - C_L) - k_{ao,H}(T_H) (C_{atm} - C_H) - P(T_L, C_{atm}) + R(T_L) C_{land} + E(t) \]

Radiative Forcing

\[ F_{CO2}(C_{atm}) = \xi \ln \frac{C_{atm}}{C_{ref}} \]

Temperature-dependent air-ocean exchange

\[ k_{ao,L}(T_L) = k_{ao,0} [ 1 - \beta (T_L - T_{ref}) ] \\ k_{ao,H}(T_H) = k_{ao,0} [ 1 - \beta (T_H - T_{ref}) ] \]
